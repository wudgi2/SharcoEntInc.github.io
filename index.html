HISTORY

Sharco Ent. originally started out as Bob Clark Trucking back in 1974 and remained so for 15 years. It worked primarily in residential site excavation, then it turned into Sharco Ent. when Sharon Clark became the owner and became a woman's business enterprise WBE.

The company started bidding on commercial projects as well as maintaining the residential side and has flourished in both. The business broadened its skills and took on other facets of the excavation industry, which led it to the need of hiring more employees.

Sharco works with general contractors and homebuilders as our quality of work and professionalism stands out amongst others. Sharco gets many repeat customers and referrals for our quality of work.
